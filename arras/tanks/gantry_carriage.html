Gantry Carriage

The Gantry Carriage is a Carriage that can sacrifice its connection to make trails in which the carriage can move without player 2. Player 2 is still required to fire its Steamroller gun.

Design

It is a Basic with a winched Carriage on its back. Said Carriage has a Steamroller turret on top.

Technical

Shift can be pressed to disconnect or reconnect the carriage.

When the carriage is disconnected, it cannot do anything without Player 2's intervention, unless there is a gantry path, in which it moves autonomously from one side to the other. Player 1 gets a minimap marker that allows them to track where their carriage is.

When the Carriage is connected, Player 1 can create a gantry path intangible to everything but Player 1's carriage.
