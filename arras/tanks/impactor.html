Impactor

The Impactor is a tank that shoots extremely fast and highly penetrating bullets. It upgrades from Ranger and Railgun at level 60.

Design

It has a team-colored circular body with two very long light gray angled supports and 8 thick light gray plates.

Technical

Shoots extremely fast bullets with good damage at a snail-like reload of 8 seconds (with 0 points in reload). Said slugs have decorative trails with very low damage. Each bullet can penetrate up to 9 tanks, deals 5x armor durability damage and ignores 75% of each enemy tank's DR.

Tier 5 Tank

Upgrades from Railgun and Ranger

Weapons:

  • 1 Railgun with 8 segments

Has same body stats as Ranger

A powerful magnetic cannon, capable of piercing armor and hull alike. Now with faster bullets.
