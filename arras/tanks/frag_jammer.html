Frag Jammer

The Frag Jammer is an Assassin that inflicts the Weakness effect, nut only on frag bullets. It upgrades from Gun Jammer at level 60.

Design

It has a team-colored circular body with a Assassin barrel, whose base has an AP Basic Barrel-shaped dark cyan prop, and a slightly wider but much shorter dark cyan inverted chevron-shaped prop betweeen the base's end and barrel's end.

Technical

Weakness Effect

The Weakness effect has 3 parameters:

  • Time, which is common to all effects,
  • Bullet Weakening, weakens the afflicted's projectiles by a percentage,
  • Ram Weakening, reduces the afflicted's body damage by a percentage.

Definitions

  • Frag Projectile - Defined as any projectile spawned from another projectile's death.

The tank itself

Basically an Assassin whose bullets inflict 60% bullet weakness to the afflicted's frag projectiles for 15 seconds.
