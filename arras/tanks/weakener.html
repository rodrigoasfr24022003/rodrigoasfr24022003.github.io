Weakener

The Weakener is a Sniper that inflicts the Weakness effect. It upgrades from Sniper at level 30.

Design

It has a team-colored circular body with a Sniper barrel and a thinner and much shorter rectangular prop on top.

Technical

Weakness Effect

The Weakness effect has 3 parameters:

This section is going to be moved to its own page after I deal with the tanks

  • Time, which is common to all effects,
  • Bullet Weakening, weakens the afflicted's projectiles by a percentage,
  • Ram Weakening, reduces the afflicted's body damage by a percentage.

The tank itself

Basically a Sniper that inflicts 15% bullet weakness and 15% ram weakness for 15 seconds.
