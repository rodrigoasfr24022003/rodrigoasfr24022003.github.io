Infernal Forge

The Infernal Forge is a fiery version of Rossio that can mine rocks to get rock and resources. It upgrades from Rossio at level 60.

Design

It has a team-colored circular body with a wide but less thin orange handle and a dark gray rectangular hammer head with golden corners.

Technical

Mining

It can mine rocks for rock and resources if it rotates 120º within 1s. It does this via an explosion with radius equivalent to 5 times the tank body and center on the hammer head.

Melee

The Infernal Forge can deal melee damage to enemy tanks, if the swing is at least 360º within 3s. However this explosion deals a bit more damage to tanks when compared to Lancer and deals triple damage against the undead. And the explosion inflicts the Torch effect to enemies.
