The Green Ion Beamis a tank that shoots a hitscan beam. It upgrades from Giga Laser Blaster, Lancer and Assassin at level 60.

Design

It has a team-colored circular body with a large gray rectangular beam medium, with a green trapezoid, tapering outwards and with the same width as the rectangle, below it and 2 green right triangular beam stabilizers, facing with their peak in the middle.

Technical

See the Ion Beam Combining page.

This tank fires a hitscan beam that deals a low amount of damage every second. It also has ramp-up and ramp-down times where it deals less damage. As it is an energy beam, it is affected by Mirror Plating.

Green Ion Beams deal equal health damage and shield damage.

Tier 5 Tank

Upgrades from Giga Laser Blaster, Lancer and Assassin

Weapons:

  • 1 Green Ion Beam Emitter

Has same body stats as Assassin

A beam cannon that deals slow but consistent damage.
