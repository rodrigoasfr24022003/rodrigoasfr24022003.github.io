Sponge

Not to be confused with Woomy's Sponge.

The Sponge is a Smasher that absorbs effects. It upgrades from Smasher at level 60.

Design

It has a team-colored circular body with holes and a wavy light yellow shell on the outside.

Technical

The tank itself

It is a smasher that can absorb effects to a certain extent.

Effect Absorption

The Sponge can absorb effects from bullets or from ramming tanks, as follows:

Exposure amount varies with size, with a tank body giving 2.5x more exposure than a Basic bullet with the effect. Values are for a Sniper bullet (1.25x exposure).

Effect Max Exposure Negative Effect on Unsafe Exposure
Freezing 5 seconds of Freezing 2 or 1 minute of Freezing 1. Freezing levels greater than 2 trigger the unsafe exposure effect instantly. The sponge crystallizes, preventing absorption until the next ram, where it releases all effect fluid as a puddle.
Acid/Poison 5 seconds of Acid 2 or 1 minute of Acid 1. Acid levels greater than 2 trigger the unsafe exposure effect instantly. The sponge starts disintegrating, slowly releasing its fluids as puddles.
Torch/Fire All exposure is unsafe. The sponge starts burning, rapidly releasing its fluids as gas.
Hellfire (Earth) All exposure is unsafe. Instant death, releases fluids and explodes in a large radius.
Weakness 5 seconds of Weakness 2 or 1 minute of Weakness 1. Weakness levels greater than 2 trigger the unsafe exposure effect instantly. The sponge disintegrates when hit, releasing fluid as puddles.
Cripple/Paralyze 5 seconds of Paralyze. Paralyzes the tank and prevents absorption and release until it is hit.
EMP All exposure is safe. None as EMP only affects barrels.
Rejuvenation (PS3/Abyss) All exposure is unsafe. Effect functions normaly.
Reverse (PS3/Abyss) All exposure is unsafe. Effect functions normaly.
Destabilize (PS3/Abyss) 5 seconds of Destabilize 2 or 1 minute of Destabilize 1. Weakness levels greater than 2 trigger the unsafe exposure effect instantly. Gradually reduces sponge's absorption rate.
Steel Unabsorbable No effect.
Beserk(PS3/Abyss) Unabsorbable No effect.
Demobrigation(Toast) All exposure is unsafe. Makes exposure to any amount of any effect unsafe.
Gravitate(Fourche) Exposure to a Gravitate effect stronger than 5g is unsafe and exposure to a Gravitate effect stroner than 15g instakills. Decreases the sponge's size.
Extreme Downforce All exposure is safe. Does nothing.
Extreme Upforce All exposure is safe. Does nothing.
Unfix(Smaragdus) All exposure is safe. Does nothing.
Infestation All exposure is unsafe. Damages the sponge without releasing fluids. The resulting infesters will have all effects of the sponge.
Mutation(Earth) All exposure is unsafe. The sponge gains tumors, (eventually) killing it.
Other Effects All exposure is safe. Does nothing.
