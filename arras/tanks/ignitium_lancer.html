Ignitium Lancer

The Ignitium Lancer that wields a fire magic lance. It upgrades from Pyromancer and Lancer at level 60.

Design

It has a team-colored circular body, a dark red giant sword blade with 2 outlines, with one of them being a translucent blaze rod-color and the other being blaze rod-colored.

Technical

Enemies hit by this lance will take 70% of a L60 Basic's HP worth of damage (per half-second) + 5s of torch.

This tank is immune to torch, hellfire and blazing brand.

It can hold the left mouse button for 1.5 seconds, creating 6 blazing brand areas in a front-facing line that also inflict regular torch for 3s. These explosions last 6 seconds each, before exploding for 140% of a L60 Basic's health worth of damage in a radius of 2 L60 Basic Body sizes. This ability has a 30-second cooldown.

  • Blazing Brand makes attacks ignore 10% of the player's damage reduction per level.

It can also press the right mouse button to send a dense 90º arc of Torch bullets that inflict Torch for 1 second, like Gust's arc, but uniform and only once. This ability has a 5-second cooldown.

Trivia

This tank is based on L Ender's Cataclysm's Incinerator.
