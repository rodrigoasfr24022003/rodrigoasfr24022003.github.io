Swarm Rocket Launcher

The Swarm Rocket Launcher is a tank that shoots fast but fragile and weak rokets at a extremely fast reload. It upgrades from Micro Rocket Launcher at level 60.

Design

It has a team-colored circular body with 2 gray Micro Rocket Launcher barrels arranged like a Uzi.

Said rockets look like short version of real rockets, having their body with 3 square segments and a triangular segment, as well as 2 fins, all team-colored and a gray rocket engine in the back.

Technical

Shoots very fast homing rockets that can be shot down with a single Gunner bullet. It has a fast reload of 0.25s, alreacy counting with the multiple barrels. Said rockets deal 25% less direct damage than their predecessors.

Tier 5 Tank

Upgrades from Micro Rocket Launcher

Weapons:

Has same body stats as Launcher
