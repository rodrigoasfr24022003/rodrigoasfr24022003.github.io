Pyromancer

This page has redlinks (links that lead nowhere). This is not an error, as I'm prioritzing porting my main tank sheet over mechanics.

The Pyromancer that can wield fire magic. It upgrades from Mage at level 45.

Design

It has a team-colored circular body, a dark red Warden Barrel serving as the staff's rod, an orange lava crystal as the rod's decoration. On the end, it has lava crystals for decoration and a lava crystal for a wand core, which hovers beyond the tip of the rod.

Technical

The Pyromancer can summon a non-homing fireball that has Pounder stats and inflicts 3 seconds of torch on right-click, at the cost of 40 mana per click.

The Pyromancer can also shoot a firestorm in a 60ยบ cone with radius equivalent to a basic FoV, that inflicts torch for 5 seconds on left-click, at the cost of 40 mana per second the left mouse button is held.

The Pyromancer has a buffer of 120 fire mana, and regenerates 1 mana per minute per 10 degrees Kelvin of temperature.
