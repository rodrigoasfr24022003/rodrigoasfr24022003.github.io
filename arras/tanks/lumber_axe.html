Lumber Axe

The Lumber Axe is a one-sided mining version of the Axeman.

Design

It has a team-colored circular body with a extremely long but thin gray handle with a spear tip on its end and a massive gray lumber axe head, represented by a quarter-circle.

Technical

General

By default only the counterclockwise direction is effective, but you can right-click to switch the effective orientation. This ability has a 1-second cooldown.

Mining

It can mine wood by touching it with the axe head. It does this via an explosion with radius equivalent to 3x the tank body and center on the pickaxe head. This axe is 27 times as efficient at obtaining wood than a regular Axeman.

Melee

Same stats as Axeman, save for the maximum turn speed of 60°/s.

Credits

Tinker's Construct for the original weapon type.
