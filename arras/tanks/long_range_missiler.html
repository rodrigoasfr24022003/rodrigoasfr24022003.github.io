Long Range Missiler

The Long Range Missiler is a tank that shoots fast but fragile missiles with extra range and 2 stages. It upgrades from Missiler and Sidewinder at level 60.

Design

It has a team-colored circular body with a wide gray Missiler barrel that has a bottom gray trapezoidal base, tapering outwards relative to the body.

Said missiles's first stage look like real missiles, having their body with 4 square segments and a triangular segment, as well as 2 fins, all team-colored and a gray rocket engine in the back.

Said missiles' second stage look like a proper second stage, having the same look as Nuker or EMP Nuker's first stage minus the warhead.

Technical

Shoots very fast and very high-range homing missiles that can be shot down with a single Basic bullet. It has a very slow reload of 5s. Said missiles deal considerable direct damage and deal moderate splash damage in a medium radius.

Said missiles have 2 stages, detaching their second stage when said stage's fuel is exhausted.

Tier 5 Tank

Upgrades from Missiler and Sidewinder

Weapons:

  • 1 Long-Range Missile Launcher

Has same body stats as Sidewinder

A long-range high-explosive missile launcher.
