Axeman

The Axeman is a Lancer that can mine wood and disable physical shields. It upgrades from Basic at level 45.

Design

It has a team-colored circular body with a long but thin gray handle with a spear tip on its end and a gray double-headed axe head.

Technical

Mining

It can mine wood by touching it with the axe head. It does this via an explosion with radius equivalent to the tank body and center on the pickaxe head.

Melee

The Axe can deal melee damage to enemy tanks, being more damaging than Lancer, with the added r-click bonus of disabling physical shields for 5 seconds.

Tier 4 Tank

Upgrades from Basic

Weapons:

  • 1 Axe Head

Inherits base body stats from Basic

A weaker mining version of Smaragdus' Brander. Does not deal much damage unless we're talking about rocks.
