Railtwin

The Railtwin is literally a twin Railgun. It upgrades from Twin Assassin at level 60.

Design

It has a team-colored circular body with a light gray large trapezoid and 2 light gray Railguns on that trapezoid.

Technical

Shoots very fast bullets with good damage, albeit a bit less than its predecessor at a less abysmal reload of 4 seconds per barrel (with 0 points in reload). Said slugs have decorative trails with very low damage. Each bullet can penetrate up to 6 tanks, deals 3x armor durability damage and ignores 50% of each enemy tank's DR.

Alternating mode

The Railtwin fires the left cannon first, then the right cannon, at a 4 second interval between railguns.

Synced mode

The Railtwin fires both cannons at the same time, resulting in a 8 second reload.

Tier 5 Tank

Upgrades from Twin Assassin and Railgun

Weapons:

  • 2 Railguns

Has same body stats as Twin Assassin

Two powerful magnetic cannons, capable of piercing armor and hull alike.
