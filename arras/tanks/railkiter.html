Railkiter

The Railkiter is a tank that shoots very fast and highly penetrating bullets. It upgrades from Railgun at level 60.

Design

It has a team-colored circular body with a Railgun barrel and 2 squares with trapezoidal thrusters on the front side, connected to the middle of the tank body by more rectangles, all of them light gray.

Technical

Similar to Railgun, except that it can enter kite mode on right-click, where it deals 20% less body damage, but has a lot of speed. Right-click again to leave kite mode.

Tier 5 Tank

Upgrades from Assassin

Weapons:

  • 1 Railgun
  • 2 Kiting Thrusters

Has same body stats as Assassin

A powerful magnetic cannon, capable of piercing armor and hull alike. Now it has an annoying level of backwards movement.
