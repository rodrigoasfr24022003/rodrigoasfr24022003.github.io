Mule

The Mule is a Basic with extra storage capacity. It upgrades from Basic at level 45.

Design

It has a team-colored half-rectangular, half-circular body with a Basic barrel. The body has "light team color" circular decoration.

Technical

Storage and inventory will be handled later

Basically a Basic with extra storage capacity.

Tier 4 Tank

Upgrades from Basic

Weapons:

  • 1 Basic Barrel
  • Extra Storage

Has same body stats as Basic, except for its speed, which is 2/3 of a basic's speed.
