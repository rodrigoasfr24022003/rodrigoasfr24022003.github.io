Missiler

The Missiler is a tank that shoots fast but fragile missiles. It upgrades from Launcher at level 45.

Design

It has a team-colored circular body with a wide gray trapezoid, tapering outwards, a wide and long gray rectangle, and 2 gray right triangles, with their "valley" in the middle.

Said missiles look like real missiles, having their body with 4 square segments and a triangular segment, as well as 2 fins, all team-colored and a gray rocket engine in the back.

Technical

Shoots very fast homing missiles that can be shot down with a single Basic bullet. It has a slow reload of 3s. Said missiles deal considerable direct damage and deal moderate splash damage in a medium radius.

Tier 4 Tank

Upgrades from Launcher

Weapons:

  • 1 Missile Launcher

Has same body stats as Launcher

A high-explosive missile launcher.
