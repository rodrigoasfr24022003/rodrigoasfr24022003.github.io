Nuker

The Nuker is a tank that shoots nuclear missiles. It upgrades from Missiler and Bumper at level 60.

Design

It has a team-colored circular body with a bigger gray Missiler barrel inscribed with a radiation symbol.

Said missiles look like real missiles, having their body with a 4x2 rectangle of square segments and a large triangular segment, as well as 2 fins, all team-colored and three gray rocket engines in the back.

Technical

Shoots initially slow and slow-turning missiles that home in on enemies for the first 2.5s of their lifespan, then these missiles accelerate massively and cease to be homing. It has a snail-like reload of 12s. Said missiles deal overkill direct damage and deal slightly overkill splash damage in a large radius.

Tier 5 Tank

Upgrades from Missiler and Bumper

Weapons:

  • 1 Nuclear Missile Launcher

Has same body stats as Bumper

A nuclear missile launcher.
