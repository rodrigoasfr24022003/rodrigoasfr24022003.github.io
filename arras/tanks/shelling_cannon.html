Shelling Cannon

The Shelling Cannon is a powerful tank at long-range but fails at close range due to its abysmal reload. It upgrades from Ranger, Shrapnel and Annihilator

Design

It has a team-colored circular body, with a long tapering inwards trapezoid that has a long rectangular barrel attached to it, which in turn has a very short tapering outwards trapezoid, which has a short and wide barrel.

Technical

Its bullets have the combined stats of Ranger's bullets and Annihilator's bullets and a decently powerful AoE with 2 Annihilator bullets of radius.

Tier 5 Tank

Upgrades from Ranger, Shrapnel and Annihilator

Weapons:

  • 1 Shelling Barrel

Inherits base body stats from Basic

Inflicts massive damage and decent AoE damage at long distances, has abysmal reload.
