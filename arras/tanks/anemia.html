Anemia

The Anemia is a Chasseur that inflicts the Weakness effect. It upgrades from Weakener and Chasseur at level 45.

Design

It has a team-colored circular body with a segmented Chasseur lance and a thinner and much shorter trapezoidal dark purple prop on top.

Technical

Weakness Effect

The Weakness effect has 3 parameters:

This section is going to be moved to its own page after I deal with the tanks

  • Time, which is common to all effects,
  • Bullet Weakening, weakens the afflicted's projectiles by a percentage,
  • Ram Weakening, reduces the afflicted's body damage by a percentage.

The tank itself

Basically a Chasseur whose lance inflicts 30% ram weakness for 15 seconds.

Tier 4 Tank

Upgrades from Chasseur and Weakener

Weapons:

  • 1 Weakening Chasseur Lance

Inherits body stats from Lancer

A Chasseur that weakens enemies.
