Railgun

The Railgun is a tank that shoots very fast and highly penetrating bullets. It upgrades from Assassin at level 45.

Design

It has a team-colored circular body with two light gray long angled supports and 6 thick light gray plates.

Technical

Shoots very fast bullets with good damage at a abysmal reload of 5 seconds (with 0 points in reload). Said slugs have decorative trails with very low damage. Each bullet can penetrate up to 6 tanks, deals 3x armor durability damage and ignores 50% of each enemy tank's DR.
