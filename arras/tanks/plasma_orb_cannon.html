Plasma Orb Cannon

This page has redlinks (links that lead nowhere). This is not an error, as I'm prioritzing porting my main tank sheet over tanks that are not on the main sheet.

The Plasma Orb Cannon is a tank that shoots energy bullets with Obliterator's stats. It upgrades from Destroyer and Mega Laser Blaster at level 45.

Design

It has a team-colored circular body with a gray wide trapezoid tapering inwards, 2 outwards-tapering trapezoids, with the bottom trapezoid having greater base thickness, a thin rectangular plate and "plasma claws" on the front. On the back it has a gray trapezoid and a smaller team-colored trapezoid.

Technical

Shoots plasma bolts with Obliterator stats, which inflict 1 second of EMP to enemies.
