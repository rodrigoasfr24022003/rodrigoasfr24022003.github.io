Charged Shotgun ([8,4,2])

The Charged Shotgun ([8,4,2]) is a tank that shoots 3 different-sized charged shots. It upgrades from Multishot and Charged Shooter at level 45.

Design

It has a team-colored circular body with a dark gray square that has a charge indicator on top that is showing 13 in base 4 in team colors and a dark gray inwards tapering trapezoid in front of it. On said trapezoid, it has a short rectangular barrel, with squares indicating 8 in base 4, 4 in base 4 and 2 in base 4 inscribed on it.

Technical

Shoots a Pounder charged shot, a Basic charged shot and a charged shot that has stats in between Basic and Pounder.
