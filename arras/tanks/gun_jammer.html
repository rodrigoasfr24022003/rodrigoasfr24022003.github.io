Gun Jammer

The Gun Jammer is an Assassin that inflicts the Weakness effect. It upgrades from Assasin and Weakener at level 45.

Design

It has a team-colored circular body with a Assassin barrel, whose base is bright purple and a slightly wider much shorter purple chevron-shaped prop betweeen the base's end and barrel's end.

Technical

Weakness Effect

The Weakness effect has 3 parameters:

This section is going to be moved to its own page after I deal with the tanks

  • Time, which is common to all effects,
  • Bullet Weakening, weakens the afflicted's projectiles by a percentage,
  • Ram Weakening, reduces the afflicted's body damage by a percentage.

The tank itself

Basically an Assassin whose bullets inflict 30% bullet weakness for 15 seconds.
