Cold Snap Staff

The Cold Snap Staff is a tank that can wield advanced ice magic. It upgrades from Cryomancer at level 60.

Design

It has a team-colored circular body, a gray staff rod. On the end, it has ice crystals for decoration and a large hollow 8-pointed star-shaped ice crystal for a wand core, which hovers beyond the tip of the rod.

Technical

The Cold Snap Staff can summon a non-homing ice ball that has Basic stats and inflicts 15 seconds of cold snap on right-click, at the cost of 120 mana per click. Said effect doubles damage taken if you already have the chill or freeze effects. Damage is tripled instead of doubled if the previous condition + the afflicted being immune to any fire-based effect applies.

The Cold Snap Staff can also shoot an icestorm in a 60º cone with radius equivalent to a basic FoV, that inflicts freezing for 5 seconds on left-click, at the cost of 60 mana per second the left mouse button is held. Oh and said cloud deals 20% of a L60 Basic's max health worth of damage per second.

The Cold Snap Staff has a buffer of 240 ice mana, and regenerates 2 mana per second per 10 degrees Kelvin of temperature below 0 degrees Celsius. The lower the temperature the more mana the Cryomancer gains.
