Cluster Missiler

This page has redlinks (links that lead nowhere). This is not an error, as I'm prioritzing porting my main tank sheet over tanks that are not on the main sheet.

The Cluster Missiler is a tank that shoots fast but fragile missiles that burn enemies and split on death. It upgrades from , Frag-(8) and Torch at level 60.

Design

It has a team-colored circular body with a gray Missiler barrel and has a long AP-Basic Barrel-shaped orange prop with a inscribed rune that means "8".

Said missiles look like real missiles, having their body with 4 square segments and a triangular segment, as well as 2 fins, all team-colored and a gray rocket engine in the back.

Technical

Shoots very fast homing missiles that can be shot down with a single Basic bullet. It has a very slow reload of 5s. Said missiles deal considerable direct damage and deal low splash damage and splash Torch for 3s in a medium radius.

Said missiles split 8 of Swarm Missiler's missiles, but their splash also inflicts Torch for 1s.

Tier 5 Tank

Upgrades from Missiler, Frag-(8) and Torch

Weapons:

  • 1 Cluster Missile Launcher

Has same body stats as Launcher

A cluster missile launcher.
