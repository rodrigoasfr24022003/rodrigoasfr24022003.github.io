AP Basic

The AP Basic is a Basic that has some armor-penetrating quirks. Especially effective against Iron-branch tanks (and other effect-immune tanks) and tanks with health greater than itself. It upgrades from Basic at level 15.

Design

It resembles a Basic but its barrel's front end has 2 30º cuts, leaving a tip instead of a half-barrel.

Technical

It is a Basic whose bullets deal 20% extra regular ballistic damage to Iron-branch tanks and other effect-immune tanks and to tanks with maximum health greater than its maximum health. It also ignores 20% of the enemy tank’s damage reduction, including armor items.
