Drill

This page has redlinks (links that lead nowhere). This is not an error, as I'm prioritzing porting my main tank sheet over mechanics.

The Drill is a Lancer that can mine rocks for resources. It upgrades from Lancer at level 45.

Design

It has a team-colored circular body with a gray converted lance and a dark gray battery receptacle.

Technical

Basically a Lancer that can mine rocks for resources at the cost of 1kW of energy per rock mined. It already comes with a full 1MW battery.

Tier 4 Tank

Upgrades from Lancer

Weapons:

  • 1 battery-powered Drill

Has same body stats as Lancer

A Lancer that (literally) doubles as a drill.
