Missile Silo

The Missile Silo is a tank that shoots fast but fragile missiles from the top of its body, leaving a temporary weakspot. It upgrades from Missiler at level 60.

Design

It has a team-colored circular body with a dark gray 6-pointed star shell and a gray missile silo comprised of 2 semicircular lids on top of the tank body.

Said missiles look like real missiles, having their body with 4 square segments and a triangular segment, as well as 2 fins, all team-colored and a gray rocket engine in the back.

Technical

The Missile Silo's body has 1.5x times as much health as its predecessor, but takes takes one-third more damage for the second the silo is open.

Shoots the same missiles as Missiler.
