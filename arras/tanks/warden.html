Warden

Not to be confused with Woomy's Warden.

This page has redlinks (links that lead nowhere). This is not an error, as I'm prioritzing porting my main tank sheet over tanks that are not on the main sheet.

The Warden is a Pounder Donut that can punch enemies. It upgrades from Bun and Lancer at level 60.

Design

It has a Smashception-sized circular body. It has a Bun Barrel anmd 2 fixed-rotation fist turrets on the sides with 3 narrower barrels and a giant non-functioning AP Basic barrel serving for a fist.

Technical

It is a Bun with Smashception size and body stats whose bullets have Trance trails and are very fast and its fists can one-shot a Smashception (or 2-shot if they are intelligent and use a physical shield, as they disable shields for 5 seconds too)

Tier 5 Tank

Upgrades from Lancer and Bun

Weapons:

  • 1 Modified Bun Barrel
  • 2 Superheavy Fists

Has same body stats as Basic, except for its speed, which is 2/3 of a basic's speed.

A Basic with a ton of extra storage.
