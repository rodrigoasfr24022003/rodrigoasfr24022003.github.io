Missile Kiter

The Missile Kiter is a Missiler with kitinmg powers. It upgrades from Missiler at level 60.

Design

It has a team-colored circular body with a gray Missiler Barrel and 2 squares with trapezoidal thrusters on the front side, connected to the middle of the tank body by more rectangles, all of them gray.

Said missiles look like real missiles, having their body with 4 square segments and a triangular segment, as well as 2 fins, all team-colored and a gray rocket engine in the back.

Technical

Similar to Missiler, except that it can enter kite mode on right-click, where it deals 20% less body damage, but has a lot of speed. Right-click again to leave kite mode.

Tier 5 Tank

Upgrades from Missiler

Weapons:

  • 1 Missile Launcher
  • 2 Kiting Thrusters

Has same body stats as Launcher

A high-explosive missile launcher, now able to be annoying.
