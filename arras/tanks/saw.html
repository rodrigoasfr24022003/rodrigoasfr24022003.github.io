Saw

The Saw is a variant of the Axeman.

Design

It has a team-colored circular body with a gray saw handle on the back and a gray saw blade on the front.

Technical

General

By default only the counterclockwise direction is effective, but you can right-click to switch the effective orientation. This ability has a 1-second cooldown.

Mining

It can mine wood by touching it with the saw head. It does this via an explosion with radius equivalent to 3x the tank body and center on the pickaxe head. This axe is 27 times as efficient at obtaining wood than a regular Axeman.

Melee

Same stats as Axeman, save for the maximum turn speed of 60°/s. Oh and it can charge via holding left-click to deal up to 2x damage to tanks.

Credits

Silent Gear for the original weapon type.

Tier 5 Tank

Upgrades from Axeman

Weapons:

  • 1 Saw Blade

Inherits base body stats from Basic

A heavier Axeman. Deals hypermassive damage to wood, but has some punch against tanks if charged.
