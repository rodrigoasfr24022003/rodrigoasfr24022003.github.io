Superheavy Charged Shooter

The HSuperheavy Charged Shooter is a tank that shoots energy bullets with Annihilator's stats. It upgrades from Annihilator and Heavy Charged Shooter at level 60.

Design

It has a team-colored circular body with a dark gray square that has a charge indicator on top that is showing 256 in base 4 in team colors and a dark gray inwards tapering trapezoid in front of it.

Technical

Shoots Annihilator bullets, called Charged Shots, that cancel out with enemy bullets of the same type and merge with allied bullets of the same type.

Tier 5 Tank

Upgrades from Annihilator and Heavy Charged Shooter

Weapons:

  • 1 Superheavy Charged Shot Barrel

Has same body stats as Annihilator

A Annihilator whose bullets merge with allied charged shots and cancel out with enemy charged shots.
