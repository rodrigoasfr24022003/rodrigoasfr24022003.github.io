Heavy Charged Shooter

The Heavy Charged Shooter is a tank that shoots energy bullets with Destroyer's stats. It upgrades from Destroyer and Charged Shooter at level 45.

Design

It has a team-colored circular body with a dark gray square that has a charge indicator on top that is showing 32 in base 4 in team colors and a dark gray inwards tapering trapezoid in front of it.

Technical

Shoots Destroyer bullets, called Charged Shots, that cancel out with enemy bullets of the same type and merge with allied bullets of the same type.

Tier 4 Tank

Upgrades from Destroyer and Charged Shooter

Weapons:

  • 1 Heavy Charged Shot Barrel

Has same body stats as Destroyer

A Destroyer whose bullets merge with allied charged shots and cancel out with enemy charged shots.
