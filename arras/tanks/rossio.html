Rossio

The Rossio is a stronger version of Smaragdus' Brander that can mine rocks to get rock and resources. It upgrades from Brander and Pickaxe at level 30.

Design

It has a team-colored circular body with a wide but thin gray handle and a gray mining hammer head.
