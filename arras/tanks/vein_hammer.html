Vein Hammer

The Vein Hammer is a more efficient version of Smaragdus' Brander that can mine rocks to get rock and resources. It upgrades from Brander and Pickaxe at level 30.

Design

It has a team-colored circular body with a wide but thin gray handle and a gray vein-mining hammer head.

Technical

Mining

It can mine rocks for rock and resources if it rotates 360ยบ within 2s. It does this via an explosion with radius equivalent to 3 times the tank body and center on the hammer head. Rocks mined by Vein Hammers drop extra resources.

Melee

The Vein Hammer can deal melee damage to enemy tanks, if the swing is at least 360ยบ within 2s. However this explosion deals a bit more damage to tanks when compared to Lancer, deals 25% splash damage and 75% splash knockback in a 9 tank body radius, and deals double damage against the undead.
