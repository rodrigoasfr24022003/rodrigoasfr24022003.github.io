Defragmentator

The Defragmentator is a Destroyer that inflicts the Frag Suppresion effect. It upgrades from Destroyer and Fracker at level 60.

Design

It has a team-colored circular body with a wide gray rectangular base and a a cyan barrel, cut into 5 barrels, each alternatingly being cut into 2 or 3 barrels.

Technical

Frag Suppresion Effect

Frag Suppresion does not suppres the base non-frag layer!

The Frag Suppresion effect has 3 parameters:

  • Time, which is common to all effects,
  • Non-Boss Suppresion, suppreses the last (non-boss suppresion amount) frag layers of non-boss enmemies,
  • Boss Suppresion, suppreses the last (boss suppresion amount) frag layers of boss enmemies. Usually more than the non-boss suppresion amount.

The tank itself

Basically a Destroyer that whose bullets suppress the last 3 frag layers of non-boss tanks and the last 5 layers of boss tanks.
