Rocket Launcher

The Rocket Launcher is a tank that shoots fast but fragile and weak rokets at a very fast reload. It upgrades from Micro Rocket Launcher and Ejector at level 60.

Design

It has a team-colored circular body with 3 gray Micro Rocket Launcher barrels arranged like a Triple Shot.

Said rockets look like real rockets, having their body with 6 square segments and a triangular segment, as well as 2 fins, all team-colored and a gray rocket engine in the back.

Technical

Shoots very fast homing rockets that can be shot down with a single Gunner bullet. It has a fast reload of 0.5s, now multiplied by the 3 barrels. Said rockets deal low direct damage and very low splash damage in a small radius.

Tier 5 Tank

Upgrades from Micro Rocket Launcher and Ejector

Weapons:

  • 3 Micro Rocket Launchers

Has same body stats as Launcher
