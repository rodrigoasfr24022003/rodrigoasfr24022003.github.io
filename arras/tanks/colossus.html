Railgun

The Colossus is a tank that shoots very fast, very highly penetrating and large bullets. It upgrades from Bulldozer and Railgun at level 60.

Design

It has a team-colored circular body with two long light gray angled supports and 6 very thick light gray plates.

Technical

Shoots very fast bullets with overkill damage (240% of a average glass cannon player's health) at a snail-like reload of 12 seconds (with 0 points in reload). Said slugs have decorative trails with very low damage. Each bullet can penetrate up to 12 tanks, deals 6x armor durability damage and ignores 50% of each enemy tank's DR.

Tier 5 Tank

Upgrades from Bulldozer and Railgun

Weapons:

  • 1 Large Railgun

Has same body stats as Bulldozer

A extremely powerful magnetic cannon, capable of piercing armor and hull alike.
