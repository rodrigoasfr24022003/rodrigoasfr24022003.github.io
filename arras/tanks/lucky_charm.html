Lucky Charm

The Lucky Charm is a Basic with a small chance of not taking damage from each enemy attack. It upgrades from Basic at level 45.

Design

It has a team-colored Acolyte body, with a Basic barrel.

Technical

Basically a Basic with a 10% chance of not taking damage from any non-Arena Closer hit.

Tier 4 Tank

Upgrades from Basic

Weapons:

  • 1 Basic Barrel
  • Extra luck

Has same body stats as Basic.

A lucky bastard of a Basic.
