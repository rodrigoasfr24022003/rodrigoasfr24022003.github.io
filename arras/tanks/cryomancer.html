Cryomancer

This page has redlinks (links that lead nowhere). This is not an error, as I'm prioritzing porting my main tank sheet over mechanics.

The Cryomancer that can wield ice magic. It upgrades from Mage at level 45.

Design

It has a team-colored circular body, a dark cyan Warden Barrel serving as the staff's rod, a cyan ice crystal as the rod's decoration. On the end, it has ice crystals for decoration and a ice crystal for a wand core, which hovers beyond the tip of the rod.

Technical

The Cryomancer can summon a non-homing ice bolt that has Basic stats and inflicts 3 seconds of freezing on right-click, at the cost of 40 mana per click.

The Cryomancer can also shoot a firestorm in a 60ยบ cone with radius equivalent to a basic FoV, that inflicts freezing for 5 seconds on left-click, at the cost of 40 mana per second the left mouse button is held.

The Cryomancer has a buffer of 120 ice mana, and regenerates 1 mana per minute per 10 degrees Kelvin of temperature below 0 degrees Celsius. The lower the temperature the more mana the Cryomancer gains.
