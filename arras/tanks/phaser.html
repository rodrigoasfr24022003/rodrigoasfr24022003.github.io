Phaser

The Phaser is a Basic that can go invisible and can temporarily phase thorugh obstacles. It upgrades from Basic at level 60.

Design

It has a team-colored circular body, with a Basic barrel and 2 gray and light cyan Trance-like constructs with 2 barrels per construct.

Technical

It is a Basic that can go invisible whilst not moving or not shooting and can r-click to phase through obstacles for 5 seconds. Said ability has a 20-second cooldown, which can be reduced to 15 seconds if it is more than 75% invisible.

Tier 5 Tank

Upgrades from Basic

Weapons:

  • 1 Basic Barrel
  • Invisibility when not moving or not shooting
  • Phasing through obstacles

Has same body stats as Basic.
