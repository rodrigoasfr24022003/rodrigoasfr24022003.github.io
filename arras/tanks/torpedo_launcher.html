Torpedo Launcher

The cket Launcher is a tank that shoots fast but fragile and weak rokets at a very fast reload. It upgrades from Micro Rocket Launcher at level 60.

Design

It has a team-colored circular body with a gray modified Micro Rocket Launcher barrel. Said barrel now has 2 trapezoids on the sides.

Said rockets look like real rockets, having their body with 6 square segments and a triangular segment, as well as 4 fins, all team-colored and a gray rocket engine in the back. Said rocket engine's nozzle is colored light cyan.

Technical

Shoots very fast homing rockets that can be shot down with a single Gunner bullet. It has a moderate reload of 1s. Said rockets 50% more damage and AoE damage compared to their predecessors, but aren't homing.

Tier 5 Tank

Upgrades from Micro Rocket Launcher

Weapons:

  • 1 Torpedo Launcher

Has same body stats as Launcher

A high-explosive non-homing torpedo launcher.
