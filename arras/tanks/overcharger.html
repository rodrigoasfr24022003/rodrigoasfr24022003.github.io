Overcharger

The Overcharger The "white hole" counterpart to Drainer. It upgrades from Plasma Orb Cannon at level 60.

Design

It has a team-colored circular body with a light gray wide trapezoid tapering outwards and "plasma claws" on the front, all light gray.

Technical

Shoots Plasma Bolts identical to the ones from its predecessor, but now they inflict an effect where they increase charge of the afflicted's emitted charged shots by 3 every second for 2 seconds, then by 2 every second for 4 seconds, then by 1 every second for 6 seconds. Does not work on charged sgots with charge greater than 2048.
