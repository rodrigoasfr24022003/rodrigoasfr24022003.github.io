Charged Sniper

The Charged Sniper is a tank that shoots energy bullets with Obliterator's stats. It upgrades from Obliterator and Charged Shooter at level 45.

Design

It has a team-colored circular body with a dark gray square that has a charge indicator on top that is showing 8 in base 4 in team colors and a dark gray long inwards tapering trapezoid in front of it.

Technical

Shoots Obliterator bullets, called Charged Shots, that cancel out with enemy bullets of the same type and merge with allied bullets of the same type.

Tier 4 Tank

Upgrades from Obliterator and Charged Shooter

Weapons:

  • 1 Charged Sniper Barrel

Has same body stats as Obliterator

An Obliterator whose bullets merge with allied charged shots and cancel out with enemy charged shots.
