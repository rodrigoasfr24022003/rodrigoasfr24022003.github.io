Model-S-Pawner

The Model-S-Pawner is a Software that is Laser Blaster-themed. It upgrades from Software and Laser Blaster at level 60.

Design

The Tank Itself

It resembles a Software but the front barrel is a Laser Blaster.

The Minion

How the minion actually looks like

It has a complicated body shape with 4 Laser Blasters and 4 Thrusters.

Technical

"Protectorate" is the official Woomy term for a Software minion.

The tank itself

It is a Laser Blaster that has a singular Laser-Blaster themed protectorate as a minion.

The Protectorate

The protectorate itself has 4 Laser Blaster barrels, 4 Bateau thrusters, which increase its speed and max speed by 30% compared to a regular Software protectorate, but only has 80% Software protectorate health.

Trivia

This tank is named after the Model-S starter from Cosmoteer.

Tier 5 Tank

Upgrades from Basic

Weapons:

  • 1 Laser Blaster Barrel
  • 1 Model-S-Pawner

Inherits base body stats from Software

Spawns an agile Laser Blaster-themed minion to fight for it.
