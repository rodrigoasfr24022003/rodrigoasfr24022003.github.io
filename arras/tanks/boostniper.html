Boostniper

The Boostniper is a Sniper that can boost its speed to double its base speed temporarily. It upgrades from Sniper at level 45.

Design

It has a team-colored circular body with a Sniper barrel and a 2 gray fuel tank rectangles with gray trapezoidal thrusters on the back, attached to the tank by more gray rectangles.

Technical

Basically a Sniper that has the alt-fire ability to greatly boost itself forwards for 5 seconds. Said ability as a 15 second cooldown.
