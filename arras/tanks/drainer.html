Drainer

The Drainer Consumes charged shots for breakfast without any milk. It upgrades from Plasma Orb Cannon at level 60.

Design

It has a team-colored circular body with a dark gray wide trapezoid tapering outwards and "plasma claws" on the front, all dark gray.

Technical

Left-click mode

Shoots Plasma Bolts identical to the ones from its predecessor, but now they inflict an effect where they reduce charge of the afflicted's emitted charged shots by 1 every second for 60 seconds or until the charged shot dies, whichever is shorter.
