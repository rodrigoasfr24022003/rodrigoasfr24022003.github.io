Advanced RNG Maniac

The Advanced RNG Maniac is a tank that shoots energy bullets than can be very strong. It upgrades from RNG Maniac at level 60.

Design

It has a team-colored circular body with a dark gray square that has a charge indicator on top that is showing 2 in base 4 in team colors and a dark gray inwards tapering trapezoid in front of it.

It also has 3 wide and short dark gray rectangles, capped on the sides by wide and very short dark gray trapezoids.

In the front rectangle, it has a light gray square on the right, which represents 2 in base 4, and 2 triangles facing left, one in the middle and one on the left, with them being gray in color.

In the middle rectangle, it has a light gray square on the right, which represents 2 in base 4, and 1 triangles facing left, one on the left, with them being gray in color.

the back rectangle only has a "target symbol" on it, consisting of a gray circular outline and 4 lines, radially arranged with their midpoints on the circular outline.

Technical

Charged Shot Variable Stats

The tank itself's base charged shot has Basic bullet stats and a value of 2, but size increases with the formula 4*log_2(n)+1, with size 8 being Basic's bullet size.

The other bullet stats increase according to the following table (properly interpolated):

Charged Shot Value Similar stats to
2 Basic Bullet
8 Pounder Bullet
32 Destroyer Bullet
256 Annihilator Bullet
1024 Decentralizer Bullet

Shoots variable stat bullets, called Charged Shots, that cancel out with enemy bullets of the same type and merge with allied bullets of the same type.

The RNG Maniac's barrel either doubles the charged shot's value, adds 2 to the charged shot's value or fires the barrel, with equal probability for all events.
