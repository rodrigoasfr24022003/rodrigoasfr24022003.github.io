Stella

The Stella is a Spike that can charge at enemies. It upgrades from Spike at level 60.

Design

It has a team-colored circular body and a dark gray 4-pointed star shell on the outside. Saisd shell becomes golden when it is charging.

Technical

It is a Smasher that can charge at Scramjet speeds, inflicting Rodrigonian Flame, which is Torch but 3x as strong, for half a second on ram whilst charging. Charges have a 20-second cooldown.
