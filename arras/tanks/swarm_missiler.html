Swarm Missiler

The Swarm Missiler is a tank that shoots fast but extremely fragile missiles at a fast rate. It upgrades from Missiler and Ejector at level 60.

Design

It has a team-colored circular body with 3 smaller gray Missiler barrels arragned like a Triplet's barrels.

Said missiles look like simplified missiles, having their body with a square segment and a triangular segment, all team-colored and a gray rocket engine in the back.

Technical

Shoots very fast homing missiles that can be shot down with a single Pelleter bullet. It has a moderately fast reload of 0.8s. Said missiles deal low direct damage and deal extremely low splash damage in a small radius.

Tier 5 Tank

Upgrades from Missiler and Ejector

Weapons:

  • 3 Micromissile Launchers

Has same body stats as Twin Launcher

AA tank that shoots volleys of 3 micromissiles.
