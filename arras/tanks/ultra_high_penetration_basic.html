Ultra High-Penetration Basic

The Ultra High-Penetration Basic is a Basic that has better armor-penetrating quirks. Especially effective against Iron-branch tanks (and other effect-immune tanks) and tanks with health greater than itself. It upgrades from AP Basic at level 30.

Design

It resembles an High-Penetration Basic but it has a white barrel further forwards from the main 2 barrels, arranged like a Minigun.

Technical

It is a Basic whose bullets deal 60% extra regular ballistic damage to Iron-branch tanks and other effect-immune tanks and to tanks with maximum health greater than its maximum health. It also ignores 60% of the enemy tankâ€™s damage reduction, including armor items.
