EMP Burst Cannon

The EMP Burst Cannon is a tank that shoots 4-8 EMP bullets per shot. It upgrades from Shotgun and Surge at level 60.

Design

It has a team-colored circular body with a gray Shotgun barrel, with a black trapezoidal prop on the Shotgun's trapezoid and 3 yellow stripe props on the Shotgun's rectangle.

Technical

Shoots 4-8 weak EMP bullets (the distribution starts more on the lower end, but progressively goes to the upper end) with a moderately slow reload of 1.5s. When fired for too long it will overheat, requiring 10 seconds to fire again. Not firing it for a while will allow it to dissipate heat whilst not overheating.
