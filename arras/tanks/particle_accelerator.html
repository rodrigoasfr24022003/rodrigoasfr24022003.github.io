Particle Accelerator

The Particle Accelerator is a tank that shoots very fast and highly penetrating bullets that ignore physical shields. It upgrades from Surge and Railgun at level 60.

Design

It has a team-colored back-cut square body with two long gray angled supports and 6 thick gray plates. It also has 2 yellow decorational rectangles on the back edges.

Technical

Shoots very fast bullets with good damage at a abysmal reload of 6 seconds (with 0 points in reload). Said slugs have decorative trails with very low damage. Each bullet can penetrate up to 6 tanks, deals 3x armor durability damage and ignores 50% of each enemy tank's DR. They also ignore physical shields completely.

Tier 5 Tank

Upgrades from Assassin

Weapons:
