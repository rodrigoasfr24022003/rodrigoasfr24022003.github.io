Painite

The Painite is a Smasher that gains speed when health is lost. It upgrades from Smasher at level 60.

Design

It has a team-colored circular body and a dark red hexagonal shell on the outside.

Technical

It is a Smasher that gains max speed on health loss, with the initial speed being 75% that of Smasher's and the speed at less than 50% health being 150% that of Smasher's.

Tier 5 Tank

Upgrades from Smasher

Weapons:

  • 1 Painite Shell

Inherits body stats from Smasher, except for sppeed, which varies.

A Smasher that accelerates when health lowers.
