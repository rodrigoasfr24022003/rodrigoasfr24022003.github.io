Chomper

The Chomper is a Akafuji that can chomp enemies. It upgrades from Akafuji at level 60.

Design

It has a team-colored circular body and a short Akafuji lance with teeth instead of a Basic barrel.

Technical

It is an Akafuji that does not deal damage on its opening animation, but deals triple damage during its closing animation.
