EMP Nuker

The EMP Nuker is a tank that shoots fast EMP missiles. It upgrades from Missiler and Surge at level 60.

Design

It has a team-colored circular body with a wide gray large Missiler barrel with 2 lightning bolts inscribed on it.

Said missiles look like real missiles, having their body being a 4x2 grid of square segments and a ornate segment with a black orb, as well as 2 fins, all team-colored and three gray rocket engines in the back.

Technical

Shoots very fast homing missiles that can be shot down with a single Destroyer bullet. It has a slow reload of 6s. Said missiles deal low direct damage and inflict EMP for up to 15s in a large radius.

Tier 5 Tank

Upgrades from Missiler and Surge

Weapons:

  • 1 EMP Nuke Launcher

Has same body stats as Launcher

A crippling missile launcher.
