Micro Rocket Launcher

The Micro Rocket Launcher is a tank that shoots fast but fragile and weak rokets at a fast reload. It upgrades from Launcher at level 45.

Design

It has a team-colored circular body with a wide gray trapezoid, with 2 trapezoidless thin Missiler barrels.

Said rockets look like real rockets, having their body with 6 square segments and a trianguler segment, as well as 2 fins, all team-colored and a gray rocket engine in the back.

Technical

Shoots very fast homing rockets that can be shot down with a single Gunner bullet. It has a fast reload of 0.5s. Said rockets deal low diect damage and very low splash damage in a small radius.
