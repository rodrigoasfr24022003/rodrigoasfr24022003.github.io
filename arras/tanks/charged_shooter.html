Charged Shooter

The Charged Shooter is a tank that shoots energy bullets with Pounder's stats. It upgrades from Pounder at level 30.

Design

It has a team-colored circular body with a dark gray square that has a charge indicator on top that is showing 8 in base 4 in team colors and a dark gray inwards tapering trapezoid in front of it.

Technical

Shoots Pounder bullets, called Charged Shots, that cancel out with enemy bullets of the same type and merge with allied bullets of the same type.
